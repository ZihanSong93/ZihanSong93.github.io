Healthy Places

It is health that is real wealth and not pieces of gold and silver.

--Mahatma Gandhi
This dynamic map was created using JavaScript with D3. The map shows world healthy places to live, and includes five varibles: PM 2.5 air pollution, cigarettes consumptions, diabetes prevalence, water resources, and life expentancy. Techniques used in this project: JavaScript, D3, html5, css. Click on the image could lead you to an interactive version of the work.
